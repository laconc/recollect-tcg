What is this game?

Recollect is a board card game played on a page. You summon spirits, bond them, and send them across a board that keeps closing in — racing to Score the most before the page goes dark.

A fading Memory

The board is a page, and the page is being forgotten. At Nightfall its edges close in. What still stands when the Memory shuts is what you keep.

Two readings

The Lorekeepers fight to keep the memory written. The Solace would unwrite it — lift a spirit away, leave no mark, call it mercy. Spirits are never destroyed; defeated, they are banished back to the page, and may be told again.

Spirits, bonds & combat

Play spirits with reach and resonance. Bond pairs to share their strength. Send them to engage, and leave your Impression on the ground you take.
