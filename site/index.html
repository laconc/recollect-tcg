What is this game?

Recollect is a competitive card game for 2 or 4 players (1v1 or 2v2), or solo against the AI. You play spirit cards onto a board and send them to claim tiles, while the board steadily closes in from the edges. Control more of it than your opponent when the clock runs out and you win the match.

A closing board

The board shrinks as the game goes on — near the end its edges close in. Whatever you still control when it closes is what counts toward the win.

Who you play

You always play the Lorekeepers, holding the board and keeping it filled. Your opponent is another Lorekeeper — or the Solace, an AI faction that plays the opposite game: erasing spirits, leaving nothing behind, and winning by how much it wipes away.

Spirits, bonds & combat

Play spirits with reach and resonance. Bond pairs to share their strength. Send them to engage, and leave your Impression on the ground you take.
