Play

▶ Launch the game How to play

Paper and ink, in the browser. You play on the board itself: tap a piece, its targets glow, then tap or drag to act. Every move is reachable by keyboard too, and the board is read tile by tile for screen readers. New here? The player guide walks it screen by screen.

Quick Play vs the AI

Start a game against the bot in one click — no waiting for a human to show up.

Host a match

When you create a match you'll set it up:

  • Mode — 1v1 or 2v2.
  • Seats — fill each with a player or a bot. In 2v2 any mix works: an ally can be a bot, an opponent a human.
  • Bot difficulty — per bot seat.
  • Faction — who you contend against (Lorekeepers or the Solace).
  • Invite — share a code for the human seats.
